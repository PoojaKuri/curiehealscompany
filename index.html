HEALOFLOX-OZ is an antibiotic combination used to treat a wide range of bacterial infections in children. It contains Ofloxacin, a fluoroquinolone antibiotic, and Ornidazole, an anti-protozoal, which work together to target both bacterial and parasitic infections.
