Introduction to Panchakarma



What is Panchakarma?

The five detoxifying procedures together are known as Panchakarma.The department of panchakarma in Sri Sri Ayurveda Hospital conducts all the five detoxifying procedures according to the classics. Ayurveda emphasizes on regular detoxification even for a disease free individual as it will help in enhancing their immunity, thus maintaining the healthy status in them.
The five panchakarma therapies are as follows:
• Basti (Enema Treatment)
• Vamana (Emesis Therapy)
• Virechana (Purgation therapy)
• Nasya (Medicated Nasal Instillation)
• Raktamokshana (Blood-letting Therapy)
